Developed an end-to-end analysis for  Reverse Logistics and E-Commerce Operations

#PROJECT OVERVIEW
This project analyzes the reverse logistics data to identify the the "Highest Loss-Generating Regions". By migrating rawdata from 'Excel' to 'MySQL' and visualizing insights in 'Power BI'. A revenue leakage of ₹11.91M was identified from 103.04K returned orders out of 791K total orders for 1(one) 'Financial Year'.

#Key Business Insights
-Revenue Impact: A total of ₹11.91M refund amounts incurred.
-Primary Driver: Elecronic products has been identified as the primary driver of revenue leakage.
-Geographical Loss Driver: Tier-2 cities emerged as our primary geographical loss driver across multiple states.
-Regional Loss Driver: Maharashtra emerged as the primary territorial loss driver, accounting for our largest chunk for ₹11.91M refund amounts.
